Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What's included in a lead magnet?

Lead magnets are valuable resources like ebooks, checklists, or templates that we create to help you capture leads. We handle the research, writing, and design.

Do I need to write anything myself?

No, our AI agents handle all content creation. However, you can review and approve content before it's published if you wish.

What if I don't have time to validate content?

Our AI agents include a Reviewer that ensures all content meets quality standards. You can also set up auto-publishing for complete hands-off operation.

Do I need technical skills to use INSYNTHIA?

No technical skills required. Our platform is designed for professionals who want to focus on their business, not on technical details.
